Appendix

Methodology, data sources and important information

Methodology in one paragraph

The Teroxx Portfolio Allocation Model v4.1 assigns each asset to a strategic tier (defensive, core, large cap, mid cap, small cap) and distributes a profile-specific budget across the universe using either equal-weight (Standard mode) or factor-weighted (Fundamental mode) allocation. The factor stack includes value-accrual signals, on-chain fundamentals and selective momentum overlays. Defensive sleeves (USDC, EURC, PAXG) are held flat against their reference currencies; no yield is implied or paid.

Data sources

  • Macro composite: alternative.me, Yahoo Finance, Binance public fapi, blockchain.info, CoinGecko global, CoinMetrics community API.
  • On-chain protocol metrics: DeFiLlama, Messari, native protocol RPCs.
  • Historical daily closes: CryptoCompare.
