Allocation Methodology

The Teroxx Portfolio Allocation Model assigns weights to crypto assets through a structured, multi-step process. Three allocation modes are available, each suited to different investment approaches.

All modes share the same tier structure: assets are grouped into tiers (Store of Value, Large Cap, Mid Cap, Small Cap) and each risk profile allocates a fixed budget per tier. The modes differ in how weights are distributed within each tier.

1. Standard Mode

Approach: Equal-weight allocation within each tier.

How it works: Each asset in a tier receives an identical share of that tier’s budget. If Large Cap has 25% budget and contains 3 tokens, each gets 8.33%.

Best for: Simple, low-maintenance portfolios. No model risk — purely diversified within each tier.

Rebalancing: Only needed when tier allocations shift (e.g., changing risk profile) or when new assets are added.

2. Factor Mode

Approach: 5-factor quantitative scoring model. Assets within each tier are weighted proportionally to their composite factor score.

FactorWhat It MeasuresRaw MetricScoring
Market Beta Volatility / systematic risk Absolute 30d price change Inverse percentile — lower volatility = higher score
Size (SMB) Market capitalization Market cap (USD) Inverse percentile — smaller cap = higher score
Value Trading activity relative to size 24h Volume / Market Cap Percentile — higher turnover = higher score
Momentum Vol-adjusted price trend (7d + 30d change) / ATH drawdown Percentile — tokens near ATH with positive returns score highest
Growth Recent acceleration 7d pace / monthly average pace Percentile — faster recent growth = higher score

Composite: Weighted sum of 5 factor scores. Weights vary by risk profile — Conservative tilts toward Beta (safety), Aggressive tilts toward Momentum and Growth.

Best for: Investors who want a systematic, quantitative tilt without fundamental analysis. Captures market-structure alpha (low-vol, momentum, size premia).

3. Fundamental Mode

Approach: Two-pillar composite model aligned with the Teroxx Research Team’s live trading strategy. Combines Value Accrual (VA) analysis with Protocol Performance (P3) metrics.

Pillar 1: Value Accrual (VA) — 70% weight

Measures whether protocol economics flow value to token holders. Uses 7 signals, each scored [-1, +1] then scaled to [0, 100]:

SignalWhat It MeasuresFormulaNeutral
Dilution Token supply overhang (2.0 − FDV/MCap) / (2.0 − 1.0) FDV/MCap = 2.0×
Supply Delta Circulating supply changes −supply_growth% / 3% 0% growth
Buyback Intensity Annualised holder yield from burns/buybacks (yield% − 5%) / 15% 5% annualised
Revenue Capture Share of fees flowing to holders holders_revenue / fees_30d 0% capture
Fee Momentum Market-relative fee growth (token_growth − median) / 50% At median
FDV / Fees (P/E) Valuation vs revenue generation log(FDV / annual_fees) vs log(100) P/E = 100
FDV / TVL Capital efficiency log(FDV / TVL) vs log(10) FDV/TVL = 10

Sector-Aware Weights: VA signal weights adapt based on token category. DeFi tokens emphasise buyback and revenue capture. L1 platforms weight FDV/TVL heavily. Privacy/PoW tokens are scored almost entirely on supply scarcity.

Sector ProfileCategoriesKey Emphasis
L1 Platform Layer 1, Layer 0 FDV/TVL (25%), Dilution (21%), Fee Momentum (15%)
DeFi DeFi, DEX, Exchange Buyback (25%), Revenue Capture (15%), FDV/Fees (15%)
AI / Compute AI, AI/Compute Fee Momentum (20%), FDV/Fees (15%), FDV/TVL (15%)
PoW / Monetary Store of Value, Payment, Privacy, Legacy Dilution (42%), Supply Delta (28%), FDV/TVL (25%)

VA Gating: Tokens are mapped to their accrual mechanism (buyback/burn, fee distribution, staking rewards, or none). Tokens with no mechanism have buyback and revenue capture signals zeroed. Large caps ($1B+) with no accrual receive a penalty floor.

Pillar 2: Protocol Performance (P3) — 30% weight

Measures real-world protocol health and network adoption via 4 growth signals:

Borrowed Growth (7d)DeFiLlamaLending demand growth (lending protocols)
Fee Growth (7d)DeFiLlamaFee acceleration — usage growth
Network ActivityMessariActive addresses + transaction count (L1s)

Red Flag Filter

A 4-pillar quality screen penalises tokens with structural risks. Tokens scoring above 60/100 on red flags receive a 50% allocation haircut:

  • Dilution Overhang (30%): High FDV/MCap ratio + low circulating float
  • Revenue Void (25%): No fees, no holder accrual mechanism
  • Valuation Absurdity (25%): Extreme P/E + thin trading volume
  • Unlock Pressure (20%): Positive supply inflation

Best for: Investors who want fundamentals-driven allocation informed by real protocol economics. This mode reflects the same analytical framework used in Teroxx’s institutional research.

Tier Structure & Risk Profiles

Regardless of allocation mode, assets are first grouped into tiers. Each risk profile sets a budget per tier:

TierConservativeBalancedGrowthAggressive
Fixed (USDC, EURC, PAXG)55%23%8%1%
Store of Value (BTC)30%35%25%20%
Large Cap10%25%35%35%
Mid Cap5%12%22%29%
Small Cap / Thematic0%5%10%15%

The allocation mode then determines how the tier budget is divided among individual assets within each tier.
